Virussweeper

By Harriet Ly Huyen Trang, 2021

How to play:

  • All the basic rules of the classic Minesweeper apply ( just that we now sweep virus instead of mines).
  • Right-click on a viral cell to flag it. Left click on a non viral cell to reveal the count of its viral neighbors. Left click on a revealed cell whose ALL neighboring viral cells have been flagged, to reveal the rest of its non-viral neighbors ( save you time left clicking).
  • You win when all viral cells have been flagged and all non-viral cells revealed. You lose when one of the revealed cell is actually a virus. With a little probabilistic deduction, you can beat the virus in no time. Good luck!
