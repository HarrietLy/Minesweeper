Virussweeper

By Harriet Ly Huyen Trang, 2021

How to play:

  • All the basic rules of the classic Minesweeper apply ( just that we now sweep virus instead of mines).
  • Right-click on a viral cell to flag it. Left click on a non viral cell to reveal the count of its viral neighbors. Left click on a revealed cell whose ALL neighboring viral cells have been flagged, to reveal the rest of its non-viral neighbors ( this saves you time left clicking, however if you flagged wrongly on non-viral cells, viral neighbor cells will open and you lose).
  • You win when all viral cells have been flagged and all non-viral cells revealed. You lose when one of the revealed cell is actually a virus. With a little probabilistic deduction, you can flag all the viral cells in no time. Good luck!
